Update 10/06/21

We have released a streamline of the user interface of Simple Posher as well as the website. Several functions have been consolidated.

  • The homepage feed sharer + regular item sharer.
  • The auto follower & unfollower.

    • These functions work the same way you are used to, but you can now share items or follow/unfollow users from any page with the click of one button.
